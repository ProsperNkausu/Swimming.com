    About Us

    Learn more about us

    We takes pleasure in the opportunity to present our proposal for provision of a Swim lessons to your members of staff within your organisation. Swim Zambia Academy operates from Northmead Secondary School and Lusaka Club in Lusaka. SZA works closely with adults and children from the ages of 2 and beyond offering swim lessons and training through use of qualified training instructors who are Commonwealth certified bronze life Savers. The coaches are headed by a level 1 and 2 FINA certified coach. Our swimming programs follow the American Red Cross Society and FINA learn-to-swim programs. Our services range from private lessons to group and school learn to swim programs offered from either our pools or the clients pool.
