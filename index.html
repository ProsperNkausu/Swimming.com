About Us

Structured swimming lessons for every age and skill level

Swim Zambia Academy operates from Northmead Secondary School and Lusaka Club in Lusaka. The academy works with adults and children from age 2 and above through qualified instructors, Commonwealth certified bronze life savers, and FINA certified coaches.

Lessons follow recognized learn-to-swim structures inspired by the American Red Cross Society and FINA programs. Services include private lessons, group lessons, school programs, and client-site training where suitable.
